How it works

You organise.
Everyone chips in.

No group chats, no awkward reminders, no Venmo confusion. You run the whole thing privately — the recipient never sees a thing.

1
You create the pool
Pick the gift, set the goal, name the occasion. You're in full control — the recipient is never notified and never sees the pool. Their surprise stays safe.
2
You share the link privately
Send a private link to contributors — friends, family, colleagues. They chip in at their own pace, leave a personal note, and you track progress in real time.
3
You make the moment
Once funded, money transfers directly to you. Buy the gift, wrap it up, and watch their face. GroupGifts.me handled the chaos — you get the glory.
